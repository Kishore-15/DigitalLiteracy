🔐General Digital Safety :-

✅ Use a Password Manager + Unique Passwords

✅ Enable 2FA on Every Account That Offers It

✅ Verify Before You Click: Check URLs & Senders

✅ Lock Your Phone & Enable Remote Wipe

✅ Think Before You Share: Audit Your Social Media Privacy

💰UPI & Financial Safety :-

✅ Never Share Your UPI PIN, OTP, or Screen—Ever

✅ Double-Check UPI Payment Requests & Beneficiary Names
